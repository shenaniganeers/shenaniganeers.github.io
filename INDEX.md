# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 26, 2025**

Shenaniganeers ("we", "us", or "our") built Moon Finder as a free, ad-supported app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our app.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
Moon Finder uses your device's GPS location solely to calculate the moon's position relative to where you are. Your location data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

### Motion and Sensor Data
The app accesses your device's compass, accelerometer, and gyroscope to power the interactive moon finder feature. This sensor data is used in real time on your device and is never stored, collected, or shared.

### Advertising Data
Moon Finder displays ads through Google AdMob. Google may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:
- Device identifiers
- IP address
- General location (derived from IP)
- App usage data

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [How Google Uses Information from Sites or Apps That Use Our Services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

### In-App Purchases
Purchase transactions for the "Remove Ads" feature are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to any payment information.

## Data Storage
Moon Finder does not maintain any servers or databases. All astronomical calculations are performed locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Third-Party Services
Moon Finder uses the following third-party services that may collect information:
- [Google AdMob](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543) — for serving advertisements
- [Apple App Store](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) — for processing in-app purchases

## Children's Privacy
Moon Finder does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app displays ads served by Google AdMob, which may collect device information as described above.

## Your Choices
- **Remove ads**: You can purchase the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase to stop all advertising and the associated data collection by Google AdMob.
- **Location**: You can revoke location permission in your device's Settings at any time. The app will not be able to calculate the moon's position without location access.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

## Contact Us
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@shenaniganeers.com.
